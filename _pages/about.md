I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. (2018 - 2023) candidate of Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (<a href="https://iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn">IIIS</a>) at <a href="https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/">Tsinghua University</a> (Beijing), advised by <a href="http://people.iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/~jianli">Dr. Jian Li</a>. I got BSc degree from <a href="https://www.bupt.edu.cn">Beijing of Posts and Telecommunication University</a> in 2018. During my undergraduate, I ranked first (1/565) and obtained the Special Scholarship (3/1600). I have much interest in speech and language processing. Recently, I mainly focus on low-resource speech and language processing for real-world applications, large-scale pre-training for NLP, and Neural Architecture Search (NAS). I have 10 papers accepted by top-tier conferences (4 of them as the first author) such as KDD and NeurIPs.
